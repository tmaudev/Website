Created at the 2015 Intel XDK GDC Game Jam at Cal Poly

Team Juicy Pong

Event

My teammates and I spent 14 hours developing the game you can play below. Our game was voted "best game" and we had the opportunity to showcase the game at the 2015 GDC.

My Contribution

Helped with game design, worked on core gameplay mechanics, and created power-ups.

Game Description

Juicy Pong is a 2-player game based on the timeless classic, "Pong"... but with a twist! As you continue playing, the game gets "juicier" with more exciting effects. The game also features an experience mechanic that will provide players with several power-ups to aid you to victory!
