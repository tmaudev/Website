Hardware

Hardware Design

Processing

BeagleBone Black

Micro-computer running Debian Linux. We utilize its faster processing power for tasks requiring low latency such as manual motor control and relaying live video feed.

Arduino Mega 2560

The ATmega328p micro-controller's AVR architecture allows us to easily read sensor data through various interfaces. Isolates sensor reading/filtering from other processes for more consistent sampling.
