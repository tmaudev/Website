Clarinet Guide

Updated by Tyler Mau for 2015-2016

General Rules

  • All vocals must be loud and clear.
  • All horn moves should be full and exaggerated.
  • Pep cheers are mandatory.
  • Pay attention to directors and drum majors.
  • Do not be late.

Horn Move Description Vocals Other Notes
Stab & Slice First 3 counts, stab forward with instrument on each count and on 4th count, spin to "slice". "Stab!" on stab and "Slice!" on slice. NA
Jousting Facing right, side step leading with left foot. Hold clarinet in right hand like a lance. NA Hold capes out with left hand.
Train Move both arms in circular motions by sides of body similar to train wheels. Make train whistle pulling motion with arm closest to the outside of the block. "Chuga chuga..." with circular motion. "Choo choo" with arm pulling motion. Train whistle to the side closest to the outside!
Rock the Baby Swing clarinet back and forth like a baby. Sing "Rockabye Baby" NA
Disco Make disco "S" motion with clarinet. Upper right to lower left. NA Hold on to middle joint.
Kayak Hold clarinet horizontally with both hands and make kayak motion. NA NA
Row the Boat Hold clarinet with both hands and place on side of body closest to the outside of the block. Make rowing motion. Sing "Row Row Row Your Boat" Similar to train, row closest to the outside.
Dig On the right, make digging motion with reed side of clarinet as the head of the shovel. "Heave!", "Ho!" Stay in step!
Shake Weight Starting in upper left, hold clarinet parallel to ground with reed facing toward back of block. Make shake weight motion in Z formation. NA Hold clarinet together!
Drum Major Hold clarinet in right hand and move up and down on each beat like a drum major baton. NA Clarinet held at 45º angle.
Do-Si-Do In groups of 2 starting from the left of the block, grab your link your left arm with your partner and dance in circles (do-si-do dance). NA Get back into position on time!
E-Brake Act as if you are pulling your clarinet back like an emergency brake on a car. Spin out of control. "EEEEEEEEEEE!!!" (Squealing brake noise) Be back into block by end of 16 counts.
Hokey Pokey Put correct arm forward, then back, then forward, then "shake it all about". Then turn around. Sing corresponding hokey pokey verse. End with "HUH!" 1st: Left arm, 2nd: Right arm, 3rd: Body
Princess Wave With your left arm at a 90º angle, rotate hand back in forth in an elegant waving motion. NA Reference: Princess Diaries
Ground is Lava Jump around like a maniac as if the ground is made of lava. Appropriate yelling. Don't scream bloody murder.
The Andrew Thrust hips quickly. No obscene noises. End with "HUH" In honor of Andrew's dancing!
The Kayla Make siren noises while waving clarinet around. "WEE WOO WEE WOO" In honor of Kayla the future firefighter!
